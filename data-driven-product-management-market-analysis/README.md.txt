# Data-Driven Product Management: Market Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze global fitness trends using Google Trends data to understand demand for digital fitness products and identify growth opportunities.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python
- pandas
- matplotlib

## 📊 Key Tasks
- Cleaned and processed time-series data
- Analyzed workout search trends over time
- Compared keyword popularity (home workout vs gym workout)
- Performed geographic analysis of fitness trends

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Workout interest peaked in 2020, likely due to COVID-19 lockdowns
- "Home workout" gained significant popularity during the pandemic
- Certain countries showed higher engagement with home-based fitness trends

## 🚀 Outcome
Provided data-driven insights that could help a fitness company decide whether to invest more in digital fitness products.
