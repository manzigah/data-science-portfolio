# Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze online shopping behavior to understand purchase patterns during peak shopping months and estimate the probability of future sales.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python
- pandas
- numpy
- scipy

## 📊 Dataset
- Online shopping session dataset including customer behavior, session metrics, and purchase outcomes

## 🔍 Process
- Filtered data for peak months (November and December)
- Segmented customers into new and returning groups
- Calculated purchase rates for each group
- Analyzed correlations between browsing behaviors
- Applied binomial probability to estimate likelihood of achieving sales targets

## 📈 Key Insights
- New customers had a higher purchase rate than returning customers during peak months
- Product-related browsing duration had the strongest correlation with other engagement metrics
- Statistical modeling showed the probability of achieving at least 100 sales in a campaign

## 🚀 Outcome
This analysis provides actionable insights for marketing teams to better target customers and estimate campaign success using data-driven probability models.
