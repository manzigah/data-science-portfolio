# Inspecting Electric Vehicle Charging Trends

## 📌 Objective
Analyze trends in electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure and vehicle sales in the United States to understand market growth and support strategic decision-making.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python
- pandas
- matplotlib
- seaborn

## 📊 Dataset
- Private EV charging data (ports and station locations)
- Public EV charging data (ports and station locations)
- EV sales data by vehicle model and year

## 🔍 Process
- Loaded and cleaned multiple datasets
- Merged public and private charging data
- Aggregated EV sales data by year
- Combined infrastructure and sales datasets
- Visualized trends over time using line plots

## 📈 Key Insights
- Both public and private charging infrastructure increased steadily over time
- EV sales showed a similar upward trend alongside infrastructure growth
- The growth in charging ports aligns closely with the rise in EV adoption

## 🚀 Outcome
This analysis highlights the relationship between EV adoption and charging infrastructure expansion, providing insights that can inform investment and strategic planning in the EV industry.
