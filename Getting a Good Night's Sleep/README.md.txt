# Analyzing Sleep Health and Lifestyle Patterns

## 📌 Objective
Analyze sleep and lifestyle data to identify patterns between occupation, BMI, and sleep quality, and uncover factors contributing to poor sleep and insomnia.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python
- pandas

## 📊 Dataset
- Sleep health dataset containing 374 individuals
- Includes features such as sleep duration, quality, occupation, BMI, stress level, and sleep disorders

## 🔍 Process
- Loaded and explored the dataset
- Grouped data by occupation to analyze sleep duration and quality
- Identified occupations with the lowest sleep duration and quality
- Analyzed the relationship between BMI categories and insomnia
- Calculated insomnia ratios across BMI groups

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales Representatives had both the lowest average sleep duration and sleep quality
- Higher BMI categories (Overweight and Obese) showed significantly higher insomnia rates
- Individuals with normal BMI had a much lower likelihood of insomnia

## 🚀 Outcome
This analysis highlights how occupation and lifestyle factors such as BMI influence sleep health, providing insights that could support wellness recommendations and health interventions.
