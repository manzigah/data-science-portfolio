# Examining the History of LEGO Sets

## 📌 Objective
Analyze the impact of licensed LEGO themes, particularly Star Wars, on the overall LEGO product line.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python
- pandas

## 📊 Dataset
- LEGO sets dataset containing set details such as year, theme, and number of parts
- Parent themes dataset indicating whether a theme is licensed

## 🔍 Process
- Cleaned datasets by removing missing values
- Merged LEGO sets data with parent themes
- Filtered licensed themes
- Analyzed the proportion of Star Wars sets among licensed sets
- Used pivot tables to examine trends over time

## 📈 Key Insights
- Star Wars accounts for a significant proportion of licensed LEGO sets
- Licensed themes play an important role in LEGO’s product strategy
- The introduction of licensed themes marked a key turning point in LEGO’s growth

## 🚀 Outcome
This analysis highlights the importance of licensed collaborations in driving product success and brand expansion for LEGO.
